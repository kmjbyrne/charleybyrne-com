## Ingredients

### Buns

- 1 tsp yeast
- 1 tsp honey
- 2 tbsp warm water
- 1/2 cup (115g) almond flour
- 2 tbsp psyllium husk powder
- 1/2 tsp baking powder
- 1/2 tsp xanthan gum
- 28g butter
- 3 large eggs (dough)
- 1 large egg (egg wash)
- 1 tsp sesame seeds

### Burgers

- 500g ground beef
- salt
- pepper
- 1 tbsp oil
- lots of cheese

### Sauce

- 1/3 cup mayonnaise
- 2 tbsp ketchup
- 2 tbsp yellow mustard
