## Ingredients

Serves 1

- 2 tbsp peanut butter
- 1 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp rice vinegar
- 1 tsp garlic paste
- 1 tsp sesame oil
- 1/2 tsp sugar or alternative sweetener
- 1/2 tsp chili flakes
- 3 tbsp water

## Method

- Warm up the ingredients in a saucepan
- Add to noodles or some protein
- Enjoy!
