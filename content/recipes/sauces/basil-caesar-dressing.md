## Ingredients

- 2 minced garlic cloves.
- 2 tsp basil or chopped fresh leaves.
- 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce.
- 3/4 cup mayonnaise.
- 1 tsp lemon juice.
- 1/3 tsp pepper.
- pinch of salt.
- 1 tsp mustard.

## Method

Put all ingredients into a food processor / blender and pulse until there are no
visible large bits of garlic or basil.

Enjoy!
