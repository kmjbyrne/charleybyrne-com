Typical ratio:

1:1:8

2 tablespoons each of butter and flour to 1 cup milk

## Ingredients

- 1/4 cup (35g) unsalted butter
- 1/3 cup (30g) plain flour
- 2 1/2 cups (600ml) milk
- 1/4 tsp ground nutmeg
- 1 cup (70g) parmesan
- 1 tsp salt and white pepper
- 1 bay leaf
