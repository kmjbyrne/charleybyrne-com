data:
  ingredients:
    - 1 cup ketchup (low sugar if preferred)
    - 2 tsp chipotle
    - 1/2 sugar or sweetener of choice
    - 30ml/1oz water
    - 1 tbsp mustard
    - 1 tbsp paprika
    - 1 tsp salt
    - 1 tsp pepper
---

## Method

1. Mix together and cook on medium heat for 10
2. Reduce heat to a simmer for another 10 minutes
