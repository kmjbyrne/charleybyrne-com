## Ingredients

- 5 tbsp Mayonnaise
- 1 tbsp rice vinegar
- 2 tsp tamari (soy sauce if not on hand)
- 1 tsp sugar or alternative sweetener
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 2 tbsp ground sesame seeds
- 1.5 tsp sesame oil
- 1 tbsp mirin (optional)

## Method

1. Mix all ingredients in a bowl

Enjoy.
