## Ingredients

### Bechamel

- 1/3 cup (75g) unsalted butter
- 1/2 cup (75g) plain flour
- 4 cups (1L) milk
- 1/4 tsp ground nutmeg
- 1 cup (70g) parmesan
- 1 tsp salt and black pepper

### Sauce

- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 large onion, finely chopped
- 1 large carrot, chopped
- 2 bay leaf
- 3 garlic cloves, crushed
- 500g beef mince
- 500g pork mince
- 2 tbsp tomato puree
- 125ml oz red wine
- 500g (1 pack) passata
- 450ml beef stock
- 1 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp black pepper
