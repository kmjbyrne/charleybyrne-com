## Ingredients

- 3-4 salmon darnes / fillets
- 3 tbsp mayonnaise
- 1/2 tsp paprika
- 1/2 tsp garlic powder
- 1/2 tsp onion powder
- 1/4 tsp cayenne
- 1 tbsp chives
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
- 1/2 tsp black pepper
- 1/4 tsp sea salt
